Problem Statement

"Every day, cities face a massive challenge — political rallies, festivals, sports events, and accidents create sudden traffic breakdowns. And right now, traffic authorities handle this completely reactively.

There's no system to predict how bad an event will be. No data-driven way to decide how many officers to deploy. And after every event ends — nothing is recorded, nothing is learned.

That's the three-gap problem we're solving. This is TrafficSense."
